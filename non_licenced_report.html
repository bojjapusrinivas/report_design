EUROPE

e-Reading devices represent the largest end-use application for e-paper displays market. e-Paper display based electronic shelf labels (ESLs) are registering healthy growth in the European nations. With gradual acceptance, and their ability to correspond to Internet of Things (IoT) and integration with servers for real-time price updates, ESLs are poised for a promising future. With plethora of applications in various sectors, including smart wristwatches, wall clocks, and in hospitals for displaying medical records, ESLs are registering huge popularity in Europe. ESLs are also being extensively used in logistics and luggage tags in logistics and air transportation industries. Smart display cards represent other potential segment for e-paper displays in Europe. With their ability to display balance amount, bonus points, one time password (OTP) and other transactions, the IC chip embedded e-paper based smart cards are gaining popularity among the Europeans. The smart display cards are also being used on stored value tickets and fiscal cards in public transportation. E-paper displays are also finding promising applications in health and medical device markets in Europe.
