SCOPE OF STUDY

Segmentation and market size estimates and projections in our market study are based on a combination of primary source feeds and an extensive list of secondary sources, providing information on the global and regional markets. A mixed methods approach is adopted as it allows triangulation of various sources to cross-validate findings and patterns including market size, growth forecasts and qualitative market trends. Although we do look at secondary research, the main determinants for our numbers would be predominantly primary research based inputs compiled from responses to our multilingual questionnaires. Secondary feeds include a comprehensive collection of published data on the industry, including regional trade associations, trade journals, company reports, press releases, and relevant literature. Most of the secondary source feeds are used only after validation from our primary research participants.

While projecting market numbers for the future years, we considered factors such as market growth trend over the past 2-3 years in developed as well as developing markets, market growth drivers, technology innovations and advancements, industry issues & trends, and economic trends, which directly or indirectly affect the market. Our primary research involves questionnaire targeting key executives worldwide. The questionnaire includes questions regarding product segments, end-use markets, market share data, growth drivers and key issues, among others. Our questionnaire data (multiple responses) is plotted by each year and product segment and uses a simplified cluster analysis. We go through a confirmatory primary research once we build the data from such responses. Based on the error tolerances (ETOL) set by our participants within the questionnaire, we place our own ETOL on such data.
